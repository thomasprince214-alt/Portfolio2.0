The Story So Far

I thrive at the intersection of structure and creativity. In the tech world, I specialize in building scalable, full-stack web applications. I love diving into database architecture, crafting clean APIs, and ensuring the front-end user experience is as intuitive as possible.

But my brain doesn't just run on code. When I step away from the IDE, I'm usually drafting mockumentary scripts. Writing comedy requires a different kind of problem-solving—figuring out pacing, character arcs, and the perfect punchline. Surprisingly, debugging a broken function and fixing a flat scene in a script require the exact same kind of analytical thinking.

Whether I'm deploying a new feature to production or pitching a pilot about the absurdities of corporate life, I bring a keen eye for detail and a deep empathy for the audience.
